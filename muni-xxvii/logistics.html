Hotel Accomodations

We encourage all conference participants to book accommodations at one of our preferred hotels, Hyatt Place Champaign/Urbana and Homewood Suites by Hilton Champaign-Urbana, where discounted rates are available. Exclusive group booking links and discount codes have been sent directly to all registered delegations to access these special rates.

Please note that
  • All delegates must be housed with their advisors.
  • Each delegation is responsible for making its room reservations directly with the hotels.
  • Discounted room availability is limited and will be assigned on a first-come, first-served basis. Delegations may not be able to secure their preferred accommodation.
  • The MTD Bus will not be operating on Sunday, April 20th, due to Easter Day. If your delegation plans to travel by train, please arrange transportation from the hotel to the Illinois Terminal accordingly.


For any questions or assistance regarding hotel accommodation, please contact MUNI XXVII’s USG of Logistics, Josephine Lee, at logistics@illinoismun.org.
