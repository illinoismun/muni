Join Us at Illinois Model UN

At Illinois Model UN, we offer a variety of opportunities to elevate your MUN experience. More importantly, we foster a welcoming community where fun, lasting memories, and meaningful connections with like-minded individuals are at the core of everything we do. Please email secretary@illinoismun.org to get our Discord link and follow our Instagram to stay updated!

  • ... and many more!
